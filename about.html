Meet the Founder

Built on Passion, Driven by Culture

Kathy Designs was born from a deep love for African fashion and a desire to make authentic, high-quality clothing accessible to everyone in Ghana and beyond. What started as a small dream has grown into one of Tema's most trusted fashion destinations.

Our founder, Kathy, built this brand from the ground up — with nothing but passion, a sewing machine and an unwavering belief that African fashion deserves to be celebrated at the highest level. Every piece in our collection tells a story of culture, pride and beauty.

Today, Kathy Designs serves hundreds of customers across Ghana — from everyday wear to weddings, naming ceremonies, funerals and corporate events. Whatever the occasion, we dress you with confidence.
